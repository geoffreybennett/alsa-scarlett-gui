# Digital I/O Availability

The availability of S/PDIF and ADAT ports depends on the Digital I/O Mode
setting and the current sample rate.

## Sample Rate Categories

- **Single-band**: 44.1, 48 kHz
- **Dual-band**: 88.2, 96 kHz
- **Quad-band**: 176.4, 192 kHz

## Scarlett 3rd Gen 18i20

This model has S/PDIF In/Out (coaxial/RCA), Optical 1 In/Out, and Optical 2 In/Out.

### S/PDIF RCA Mode (Default)

| Sample Rate | S/PDIF In | S/PDIF Out | Optical 1 In | Optical 1 Out | Optical 2 In | Optical 2 Out |
|-------------|-----------|------------|--------------|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| Single-band | S/PDIF 1-2 | S/PDIF 1-2 | ADAT 1-8 | ADAT 1-8 | - | ADAT 1-8 |
| Dual-band | S/PDIF 1-2 | S/PDIF 1-2 | ADAT 1-4 | ADAT 1-4 | - | - |
| Quad-band | S/PDIF 1-2 | S/PDIF 1-2 | - | - | - | - |

### S/PDIF Optical Mode

| Sample Rate | S/PDIF In | S/PDIF Out | Optical 1 In | Optical 1 Out | Optical 2 In | Optical 2 Out |
|-------------|-----------|------------|--------------|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| Single-band | - | S/PDIF 1-2 | ADAT 1-8 | ADAT 1-8 | S/PDIF 1-2 | S/PDIF 1-2 |
| Dual-band | - | S/PDIF 1-2 | ADAT 1-4 | ADAT 1-4 | S/PDIF 1-2 | S/PDIF 1-2 |
| Quad-band | - | S/PDIF 1-2 | - | - | - | - |

### Dual ADAT Mode

| Sample Rate | S/PDIF In | S/PDIF Out | Optical 1 In | Optical 1 Out | Optical 2 In | Optical 2 Out |
|-------------|-----------|------------|--------------|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| Single-band | - | S/PDIF 1-2 | ADAT 1-8 | ADAT 1-8 | - | ADAT 1-8 |
| Dual-band | - | S/PDIF 1-2 | ADAT 1-4 | ADAT 1-4 | ADAT 5-8 | ADAT 5-8 |
| Quad-band | - | S/PDIF 1-2 | - | - | - | - |
